Device configuration for Fairphone (Gen. 6)
=========================================

The Fairphone (Gen. 6)  (codenamed _"FP6"_) is a high-end smartphone from Fairphone.

It was globally released on June 25 in 2025.

## Device specifications

Basic   | Spec Sheet
-------:|:-------------------------
Platform | Snapdragon 7s Gen 3 (SM7635)
RAM & Storage | 8GB/256GB (LPDDR5 RAM, UFS 3.1 storage)
Shipped Android Version | 15
Battery | Removable, 4415 mAh
Display | 6.31″, 120Hz LTPO, 1116x2484 (431 ppi)
Rear camera 1 | 50MP wide angle
Rear camera 2 | 13MP ultra wide-angle
Front camera | 32MP in-display
